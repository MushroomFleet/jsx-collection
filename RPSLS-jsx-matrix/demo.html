5-Dimensional Player Stats Visualizer

🎮 About This System

This interactive visualization demonstrates a 5-dimensional stat system based on the Rock-Paper-Scissors-Lizard-Spock game invented by Sam Kass and popularized by The Big Bang Theory. Each dimension represents a combat affinity, and adjusting one stat automatically rebalances others based on the game's mathematical relationships.

How it works: When you increase a stat (e.g., Rock), the system automatically decreases the stats it beats (Scissors and Lizard) while maintaining total combat power equilibrium. This creates strategic depth where players must balance their affinities carefully.
